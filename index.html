About Poet Resource Hub

Hi there! I am a real human from Southwest Missouri.. And I am into poetry! I have my blog published here with poems and other content related to my life, as well as a database of resources related to poetry. Hope you enjoy my site!

Mission

To write poetry, about poetry and to try and keep a database of useful links related to poetry available to the public.
